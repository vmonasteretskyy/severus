Sound Familiar?

The problem is rarely the software. It's the operation
underneath it that nobody's properly sat down and sorted.

Your people are sharp. They're also buried in admin they shouldn't be anywhere near.

Sign-offs over email. Numbers copied between three different spreadsheets because nothing talks to anything else. Reports rebuilt from scratch every week. Everyone's genuinely grafting. The business still isn't getting anywhere.

You've bought the tools. They've made things worse

Procore for projects. COINS in the back office. Bluebeam for drawings. Sage handling the numbers. WhatsApp for everything the other four can't. Each one technically works. Together they're a mess. You've spent real money arriving at it.

You were told AI would sort it. It didn't.

You've done the demos. Read the case studies. Maybe even run a pilot that produced a deck and a few vague "insights." The ROI was nowhere. The clarity was worse. You're still none the wiser about what AI actually does for a business like yours.

You need a straight answer on whether to build, buy, or automate. Everyone you've asked so far is selling something.

Dev shops want to build you something bespoke. SaaS vendors want you locked into a 12-month contract. Every AI consultancy you speak to is pitching a transformation programme before they've spent five minutes understanding what your operation actually does. Nobody starts with the honest question: do you even need this?

When operations start pulling in different directions, progress slows.
Here’s how we bring things back into alignment.

Common Questions

What does Severus do?

Severus is a business automation and operations consulting firm. We evaluate how your business runs, find the inefficiencies costing you money, and design the workflows or technical requirements to fix them. We are not a dev agency. We make sure the right decisions get made before anyone writes a line of code.

How is Severus different from a software development agency?

A dev agency builds what you tell them to build. Severus asks whether what you want to build is actually the right solution. We are vendor-independent consultants. Our goal is to solve your business problem, sometimes that means building software, sometimes it means fixing a broken process with existing tools.

Do I need to be technical to work with Severus?

No. The majority of our clients are non-technical founders and business owners. We translate your business goals into clear technical requirements so you don’t have to guess or learn to code.

How does Severus approach AI implementation?

Severus evaluates AI only through the lens of ROI. We ignore the hype and look at your operations to see if AI can actually reduce hours spent on manual tasks, cut costs, or improve accuracy. If it doesn’t make financial sense, we won’t recommend it.

What industries does Severus work with?

Non-technical founders, SaaS companies, construction and PropTech, operations-heavy SMBs, funded startups, and enterprise operations teams. The specific industry matters less than the problem: fragmented operations, high manual effort, and scaling bottlenecks.

What does a typical engagement with Severus look like?

Most engagements start with a discovery call and an operations audit. We then map out your current state, identify the bottlenecks, and deliver a clear technical specification or automation roadmap. We can also manage the RFP process if you need to hire developers to execute the plan.
